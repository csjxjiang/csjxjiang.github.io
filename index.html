Biography

Jiaxin Jiang is now a research fellow working with Prof. Bingsheng He in School of Computing, National University of Singapore. His research interests are mainly in distributed graph database systems, big data analytics and graph anomaly detection. Before that, he received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Hong Kong Baptist University University, supervised by Prof. Byron Choi and Prof. Jianliang Xu in 2020 and B.E. degree in Computer Science from Taishan College, Shandong University in 2015 (Taishan College operates a 'pilot class' under the guidance of the MOE, selecting 15 students from an annual pool of 10,000 freshmen). He worked closely with Prof. Wenfei Fan and Dr. Jingbo Xu from 2015 to 2017.
